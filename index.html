Proposition: Filing a police complaint is worthless, as they lead to no actual justice or change.

FOR the Proposition

I used a Sankey Diagram inspired by the Minard graph to visualize the journey of a civilian police complaint from when its filed to its outcome.

Design Decisions and Rationale:

  • I used the width of the flow lines to indicate the quantity of complaints that went to each outcome. Thicker lines indicate that more complaints went to that outcome. By showing how many complaints are disregarded with a really thick line and how few complaints result in charges with a really thin line, I hope to convey how a majority of complaints are disregarded or result in minimal punishment. I dishonestly put extra emphasis on this by making all the flows to punitive outcomes even thinner than they should be and the other flows thicker.
  • Area is not the most effective visualize channel, so I used color of the flow lines as a redundant encoding for the quantity of complaints that went to each outcome. Deeper blues indicate higher quantities. The deep blues make the thick flow lines look bolder, while the light blues make the thin flow lines look even thinner. I performed the same dishonest techniques with this encoding as I did with the flow line width.
